Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide range of TV shows, movies, documentaries, and original content. Users can watch content on-demand, with the ability to stream on various devices like smartphones, smart TVs, tablets, and computers.
Netflix offers different subscription plans with varying prices:
  • Basic Plan: $6.99/month (ad-supported, 1 screen at a time)
  • Standard Plan: $15.49/month (no ads, 2 screens at a time)
  • Premium Plan: $19.99/month (no ads, 4 screens at a time)
Prices may vary by country and are subject to change.
To cancel your Netflix subscription:
  1. Go to the Netflix website and log in to your account.
  2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner and select Account.
  3. Under the Membership & Billing section, click on Cancel Membership.
  4. Follow the instructions to confirm cancellation.
You can continue watching until the end of your billing period.
